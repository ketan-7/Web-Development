StrayDogCare100

StrayDogCare100 is one of the world’s largest no-kill dog sanctuary & hospital, with 8,000+ life-critical rescues and hundreds of resident dogs. We are located in Pune, India, but we look out for dogs all across the country.
We also run various campaigns (i.e. ‘Feed Stray Dogs’ campaign) and offer grants to individuals and NGOs so that we can help as many dogs as possible. You can support StrayDogCare100 with a purchase – every purchase saves a dog!

Our Team

We are a 100% volunteer based organization. All of the donations go directly to helping the street dogs of the world. We rely on the help of the people who live local to these animals and have no paid employees.

Be an Advocate

We are always looking for compassionate people to help with our mission. We desperately need rescue partners, who can take street dogs into their programs as well as monthly sponsors and donors. We also need advocates who can bring awareness about the work we do.

Our Goals

Our goal is to help stray dogs in need. We support local street rescuers who love and care for the dogs in their local community, but do not have the resources to do so. We provide food, medicine, shelter and emergency vet care. We also support a number of shelters and rehome dogs in the US & Canada through our partner network.

Support StrayDogCare100

StrayDogCare100 is India’s first, largest, best-designed and best-managed dog sanctuary. We add to our rescued dogs at the rate of over 200 dogs a year that come by air, road and train from over 30 cities across India. For a dog that has no hope and no home across India, there is StrayDogCare100.
