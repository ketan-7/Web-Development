Our Dogs

Stray Dog Support has over 200 dogs in our program globally. We support these animals by providing each dog with needed medical care, food, boarding assistance, spay and neuter and vaccinations. Stray Dog does not offer a direct adoption program, but rather work with our partner rescues in the United States and Canada for placement once they are ready. We also fund and coordinate all of the logistics and transportation to get them abroad and into homes. Stray Dog is responsible for each dog we take into our program from the moment they are rescued until they are placed with one of our partners. We treat each dog as being part of the Stray Dog family and provide 100% of their care. All of the dogs shown here are in our program currently and will be available for adoption in the future through one of our rescue partners. If you are interested in sponsoring any of our dogs we would appreciate it.
